Pricing & Plans — Acme Cloud Platform
=======================================

Plan Overview
-------------
Acme Cloud offers three tiers designed for different business needs:

1. Starter Plan — $29/month
   - Up to 5 team members
   - 10,000 API calls/month
   - 1 GB storage
   - Email support (48-hour response)
   - Basic analytics dashboard
   - Community forum access

2. Professional Plan — $99/month
   - Up to 25 team members
   - 100,000 API calls/month
   - 10 GB storage
   - Priority email support (12-hour response)
   - Advanced analytics & reporting
   - Webhook integrations
   - SSO (SAML 2.0)
   - 99.9% uptime SLA

3. Enterprise Plan — Custom pricing (starting ~$499/month)
   - Unlimited team members
   - Unlimited API calls
   - 100 GB+ storage (scalable)
   - Dedicated support manager
   - Phone & chat support (1-hour response)
   - Custom integrations
   - Advanced security (SOC 2, HIPAA compliance)
   - 99.99% uptime SLA
   - On-premises deployment option
   - Quarterly business reviews

Annual Billing Discount
-----------------------

- Starter: $278/year (save $70)
- Professional: $950/year (save $238)
- Enterprise: Contact sales for annual pricing
